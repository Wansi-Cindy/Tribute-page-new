Carrot Juice

This is a glass of carrot juice!

How to make carrot juice:

  • Wash your hands
  • Wash the carrots
  • Grater then on the tiniest holes of the grater
  • Make sure you grater them in a bowl
  • Try to shifter the carrots to separate the juice from the chaff
  • Press the chaff to release more juice
  • Serve chilled!
